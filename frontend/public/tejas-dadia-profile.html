About Tejas Dadia

Mr. Tejas Dadia, with over a decade of experience in real estate, has established himself as a forward-thinking leader with a flair for creating innovative spaces. From resort villas to co-working and co-living concepts, his journey reflects a passion for developing projects that go beyond infrastructure to deliver real solutions for modern lifestyles.


Recently honored with the "Best Emerging Brand of the Year" award for The Cube Club, Mr. Dadia continues to set new benchmarks in Gujarat's real estate sector. His vision is clear: to transform the landscape with socially-driven, solution-oriented projects that seamlessly blend luxury, sustainability, and community living.


A true visionary, Mr. Dadia is shaping the future of real estate— where every project is not just a development, but a lifestyle revolution.
